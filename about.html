A little about me
# Hi, I'm Harsha

I am an engineer and did my undergraduate (ahem! with honors) from the Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur. I spent the past seven years perilously prancing across disciplines in search of my vocation, and fortunately, I think I have found it! - a problem worthy of time, and I am currently working on it. However, in the process, I extensively probed various disciplines such as software development, computer-aided design & engineering, machine learning, macro-economics, robotics (specifically aerial robotics), computational neuroscience, and even visual effects. Therefore, even though I majored in Mechanical Engineering (with a specialization in Mechanical Systems Design), I have worked on inter-disciplinary projects and assumed roles that I couldn't afford merely based on my [formal] academic background.

I spend most of the waking time working on my project. However, when I do make time to recuperate, I usually spend it: listening to music, reading-up on recent developments in space & defense research, international politics (I especially find it captivating to connect-the-dots between 20th-century world-history and its modern-day repercussion), or play computer games (real-time strategy is my poison!). I also enjoy reading informative books on climate change, the human brain, energy generation, and human history,... to name a few.
