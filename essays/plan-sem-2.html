My 1st semester at IIT Delhi has finished. Although it spanned four months, I must admit I didn't properly learn anything. The curriculum seems to have been deliberately structured to merely give a taste of various subjects. And I think this is because they are primarily designed with UG students in mind; these students don't need depth but rather breadth.

However, breadth is not what I need! I need depth. And this calls for a "depth-first" self-study session.
